MAXPOINT IN ACTION

ADVERTISER:

A national grocery store chain

OBJECTIVE:

Pull shoppers away from its competition See how we would help

MaxPoint would use its proprietary interest data to locate the Digital Zips® around the chain's stores where shoppers are likely to switch from the competition.

We would deliver digital display, video, and mobile ads on brand-safe sites to shoppers in the selected Digital Zips.

Our sophisticated technology would monitor the campaign around the clock for optimal responses, landing page interactions, and responder profiles.

MaxPoint would provide detailed analytics on campaign performance, including response rates, landing page view rates, website interaction rates, and audience profiles.

Of course, every campaign is unique. MaxPoint can help you find the right strategy to reach your goals. Contact us to learn more.
