How did Naked Pizza use local online advertising to increase orders of its
all-natural pizza?

Goal: Generate awareness, increase traffic, and drive sales at
select New Jersey Naked Pizza locations.

Strategy: MaxPoint used its Digital Zip® technology to target
families in the local area with an interest in maintaining a
healthy lifestyle—a perfect fit for Naked Pizza, which offers a
full menu of all-natural pizzas. MaxPoint also displayed the
restaurant’s ads to businesspeople who frequent area
establishments during the midday lunch rush.

Results: By using MaxPoint’s local online advertising solution,
Naked Pizza was able to gain new insights into its customers
making online orders, including their age, household income, net worth, and level of education. The restaurant was
also able to get valuable data about which local neighborhoods visit its online ordering page and at what time of
the day they place most orders. In all, targeted online ads have helped Naked Pizza increase sales by 19% at
one New Jersey location and generate more than 600 new visits.
