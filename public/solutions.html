What Is Hyperlocal Advertising?

Hyperlocal advertising (n):
Advertising targeted at neighborhood shoppers via local media sources, usually with a limited scale.

MaxPoint hyperlocal advertising (n):
Advertising that goes beyond local media sources to target shoppers in precisely defined Digital Zip neighborhoods. This gives MaxPoint the ability to reach crowds of ideal shoppers while still retaining the precision of a hyperlocal campaign.
